This page is dedicated to my favorite resturants in the Louisville area.

I love to eat out and try new places, so here are some of my top picks:

  1. K-Pot -- This resturant is an all you can eat buffet. It is a Korean Hot Pot and BBQ resturant. I am usally at this resturant every 2 weeks because the price and how good their food is.
  2. Lee Moo -- This resturant has a good menu from brunch to dinner. Althought this resturant is quite expensive but the food does make up for it. I typically order the Rib Eyes steaks and it is juicy and tender!
  3. Steak & Bourbon -- I would say this is 2nd in terms of steakhouse resturants in Louisville. The bourbon selection is why I classify this as one of my top resturants to eat at and drink at. The menu isnt too overwhelming like Cheese Cake factory but they have a lot of things to try. The steak is almost if not similar to Lee Moos.
